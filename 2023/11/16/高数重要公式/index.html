    1. 等价无穷小

    等价无穷小

    前提：\(x \rightarrow 0\)

    \(\ln(1+x) \thicksim x\)，\(x - \ln(1+x) \thicksim \frac{1}{2} x^2\)

    \(e^x - 1 \thicksim x\)，\(a^x - 1 \thicksim x \ln{a}\)

    \(\begin{cases} 当\ a \neq 0，(1 + x)^a - 1 \thicksim ax，(1 + x)^n - 1 \thicksim nx \\ 当\ A(x) \rightarrow 0\ 且\ A(x)B(x) \rightarrow 0，[1 + A(x)]^{B(x)} - 1 \thicksim A(x)B(x) \end{cases}\)

    \(\sin{x} \thicksim \arcsin{x} \thicksim \tan{x} \thicksim \arctan{x} \thicksim x\)

    \((x - \sin{x}) \thicksim (\arcsin{x} - x) \thicksim \frac{1}{6} x^3\)

    \((\tan{x} - x) \thicksim (x - \arctan{x}) \thicksim \frac{1}{3} x^3\)

    \(\begin{cases} 1 - \cos{x} \thicksim \frac{1}{2} x^2 \\ 1 - \cos^a{x} \thicksim \frac{a}{2} x^2 \end{cases}\)
